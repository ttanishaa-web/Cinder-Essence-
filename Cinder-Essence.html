Our Story

Cinder was born from a fascination with fire's dual nature—both destructive and regenerative, dangerous yet mesmerizing. Our founder, Isabella Blackwood, spent years studying the ancient art of perfumery across the globe before creating fragrances that capture fire's many facets.

Each Cinder fragrance is meticulously crafted in our Paris atelier, where traditional techniques meet modern innovation. We source the finest raw materials from sustainable producers worldwide, ensuring every bottle contains not just scent, but story.

Elemental Ingredients

We source the finest raw materials from around the world, each selected for their unique ability to evoke different aspects of fire's complex character.

Oud

Rare and precious, oud embodies the smoldering intensity of inferno. Harvested from agarwood trees, this resin forms only when the wood is infected with a specific mold—a transformation through adversity.

Blood Orange

The vibrant essence of flare captured in citrus form. Our blood oranges are hand-harvested from Sicilian groves, where volcanic soil infuses them with distinctive character and intensity.

Sandalwood

The heart of ember's warmth. Our sustainably harvested Australian sandalwood provides a creamy, comforting base that evokes the lingering warmth of dying embers.

Burnt Sage

The spiritual essence of ash. Our white sage is hand-harvested and carefully charred to capture the cleansing, purifying qualities of sacred smoke rituals.
